Cheeseburger

Description: This delicious looking burger is a double cheeseburger with chopped grilled onions. The onions are optional.

Ingredients:

  • Buns either homemade or store bought
  • Patties, also homemade or store bought
  • Your preferred cheese
  • Onions(optional)
  • Ketchup

Steps:

  1. Chop onions so that you made have them ready if you decide to add them to your cheeseburger
  2. Place 2 patties on the grill and set the heat to medium. Let each side cook for about 6 minutes each
  3. You may also place the onions on the grill a minute or two before flipping the patties
  4. Place your buns on the grill about 2 minutes after you've flipped your patties, if you prefer toasted buns
  5. Once you are satisfied with the look of your patties you can place a slice of cheese on each.
  6. Stack the patties on each other and place the grilled onion on top.
  7. Place the toasted buns on a plate and use a spatula to place your patties on the bottom bun then cover with the top bun.
  8. Voila! You may now enjoy your double cheeseburger!
