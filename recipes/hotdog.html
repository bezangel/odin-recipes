Hotdog

Ingredients:

  • Hotdog buns
  • Sausage links or turkey dogs
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Relish

Steps

  1. Place a skillet on stove and set it to medium.
  2. Place buns and links on skillet for about 4-5 minutes until cooked to your liking.
  3. Once cooked place bun onto a plate and place the link inside bun.
  4. Top with your preferred condiments.
  5. Ready to eat!
