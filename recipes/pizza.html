Pizza

Description: This is a simple pepperoni pizza!

Ingredients:

  • Pizza crust
  • Pizza sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Pepperoni

Steps

  1. Preheat over to 350 degrees for 15 minutes. You can complete the next steps as you wait.
  2. Place pizza crust on a pizza board or tray.
  3. Pour as much pizza sauce as you'd like on the crust and spread it in a circular motion with a spoon.
  4. Spread choose all over the sauce. You can go light or place as much as you'd like.
  5. Please pepperoni slices evenly throughout the pizza.
  6. Here you can pour more cheese on top of the pepperoni if you'd like double cheese.
  7. Put into oven and let bake for about 8 minutes. Check periodically
